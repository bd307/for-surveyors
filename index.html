Our Proven Process

A Workflow Built for
Surveying Professionals

Every project follows a disciplined, documented workflow designed to meet or exceed ASPRS positional accuracy recommendations for UAS data acquisition. We coordinate with your licensed surveyors to integrate their GCP measurements and checkpoint verification, ensuring your firm retains full control of the survey-grade validation process while we handle the aerial acquisition and processing.

01
Project Scoping & Control Planning

We review your project specs, required accuracy class, and terrain complexity. We coordinate with your team on GCP placement strategy to meet ASPRS Edition 2 (2024) control requirements. Your licensed surveyors establish and measure GCPs using RTK GNSS; we integrate those measurements into our processing workflow to ensure defensible accuracy validation.

Day 1
02
Ground Control & Flight Acquisition

Flight parameters — altitude, overlap, GSD — are calculated to achieve your required accuracy class. We coordinate timing with your survey team to align with GCP establishment. All flights are conducted by FAA Part 107 certified operators using Matrice 400 platforms with precision sensors.

Day 1–2
03
Data Processing & QA/QC

Raw data is processed through our professional-grade photogrammetry and LiDAR processing pipeline. We validate RMSE horizontal, RMSE NVA (Non-Vegetated Vertical Accuracy), swath-to-swath precision, and seamline mismatch against your target accuracy class.

Day 2–4
04
Accuracy Report & Delivery

You receive a full accuracy report with RMSE statistics, checkpoint verification, and QA documentation — not just the data files. Deliverables are formatted to integrate directly with your CAD and GIS software. No reformatting. No surprises.
